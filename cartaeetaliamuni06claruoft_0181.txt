TRANSLATION OF A FINAL GONOORD WHEREBY ROGER
WILLIAMS REOOVERS AGAINST THOMAS GRIFFITH AND
ELIZABETH HIS WIFE LANDS IN YSTRADYVODUOK.

11 APRIL. 8 WILL. ΠΙ. 1696.

This is the final agreement made in the court of the Lord the King of his
great session of the oounty of GLAMORGAN held at CARDIFF in the eounty
aforesaid on Saturday the eleventh day of April in the eighth year of the reign
of the Lord W1LL1AM the Third after the Gonquest King of ENGLAND ScoTLAND
FRANCE and IRELAND Defender of the Faith &a Before WILLIAM PovaETT
Serjeant-at-law and EDWARD SMYTH esquire justioes and other faithful people
of the Lord the King then there present Between RoGER WILLIAMS gentleman
plaintiff and ΤΗοΜΑο GRIFFITH gentleman and ELIZABETH his wife deforciants
of one messuage one barn one garden ten aeres of land thirty aeres of meadow
twenty aeres of pastnre and a hundred and twenty aeres of furze and heath with
appurtenanoes in the parish of YSTRADYVODUCK whereof a plea of oovenant was
summoned between them in the same court to wit that the aforesaid THOMAS and
ELIZABETH aoknowledged the tenements aforesaid with appurtenanoes to be the
right of the same ROGER as those which the same Roman has of the gift of the
aforesaid THOMAs and ELIZABETH And those they have remised and quitolaimed
for themselves and the heirs of the same THOMAs to the aforenamed ROGER and
his heirs for ever And moreover the same THOMAs and ELIZABETH have
granted for themselves and the heirs of the same THOMAS that they will warrant
the tenements aforesaid with appurtenanoes to the aforenamed ROGER and his
heirs against all men for ever And for this aoknowledgment remise quitolaim

warrant fine and agreement the same ROGER has given to the aforenamed

THOMAS and ELIZABETH a hundred and sixty pounds sterling.
